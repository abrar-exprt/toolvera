Frequently Asked Questions

Is this background remover really completely free?+

Yes, 100% free with no limits. ToolVera uses the open-source @imgly/background-removal library which runs an AI model directly in your browser using WebAssembly. No API credits, no subscriptions, no free tier limits. Free forever.

Does my image get uploaded to a server?+

No. Your image never leaves your device. The AI model downloads to your browser and runs locally using WebAssembly and your CPU/GPU. ToolVera's servers never see your images. The tool even works offline once the model is cached.

What image formats are supported?+

You can upload PNG, JPG, JPEG, WEBP, and HEIC (iPhone format) images up to 20 MB. Output is a high-resolution transparent PNG. You can also export as JPG or WEBP using the settings panel.

Why does it take longer the first time?+

On first use, the AI model (~40 MB) downloads from a CDN. This takes 5-15 seconds depending on your connection. After that, the model is cached in your browser and subsequent images process in 2-5 seconds.

How does quality compare to Remove.bg?+

The @imgly/background-removal library uses a modern ONNX segmentation model that produces excellent results for portraits, products, and common subjects. Plus it's completely free with no limits, making it ideal for everyday use.

Is ToolVera available in USA, UK, Canada, and Australia?+

Yes, ToolVera is available globally with no geographic restrictions. Widely used across the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, Germany, France, India, and worldwide. Browser-side processing means no server location limitations.
