How to Use the Free GST Invoice Generator

Creating a GST invoice for your J&K business takes less than 2 minutes with ToolVera:

  1. Enter your business name, GSTIN, and address
  2. Add your client's details
  3. Add the items or services you are billing for
  4. Select GST type — CGST+SGST for intra-state J&K transactions, IGST for inter-state
  5. Click Download Invoice PDF — done

JKGST — What J&K Businesses Need to Know

Jammu & Kashmir became a Union Territory on October 31, 2019, which changed how GST is applied. J&K GSTINs start with 01 (for Jammu) or 02 (for Kashmir). For sales within J&K, you charge CGST + SGST (UT GST). For sales to businesses outside J&K, you charge IGST.

For official J&K GST information, visit the J&K GST Department portal ↗ or the national GST portal ↗.
